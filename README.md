# MoodCanvas

MoodCanvas is an engaging web app that turns your daily emotions into beautiful, shareable digital art. Simply select your mood, choose colors that resonate with your feelings, and watch as the app generates a unique, abstract artwork that represents your emotional state. Share your mood art on social media or keep a private gallery of your emotional journey.

Key Features:
- Interactive mood selection with emoji reactions
- Real-time generative art based on user input
- Color palette customization
- Daily streaks and achievements
- Private gallery of mood artwork
- Social sharing capabilities

Tech Stack:
- HTML5 Canvas for artwork generation
- Vanilla JavaScript for interactivity
- CSS3 animations
- LocalStorage for progress tracking
